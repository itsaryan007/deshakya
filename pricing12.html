FAQ

How do i book you?

Once you decide if you want me to capture your memories, find the "CONTACT" page at the top, and fill out the form or you can email me directly at blonegallery@gmail.com

what types of sessions do you offer?

There is not a single thing that I don't photograph. I do portraits, maternity, families, events, cake smash, engagements, weddings, concerts, and lifestyle work. if you can think of it, I most likely take photos of it. i love growing with my clients and becoming their forever photographer.

Do you give out RAW files?

"Chefs don't serve raw pork, and photographers don't serve RAW files. Because of safety.

How long will take it take to get the image back?

I ask for up to 5 weeks to deliver a full wedding gallery. For any type of portrait session, I ask for up to 1 - 2 weeks.

Can we do more than 1 location?

Yes! there will be added cost to take into account the travel time between each location and the extended time to fully capture the shots at each location. This works best in San Francisco!
